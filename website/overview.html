Overview

Our project is an archway of blooming flowers that follow and open as you pass under the arch.

FILL IT IN

Electrical

Consisting of ten (10) servos, five (5) VL53L0X time-of-flight sensors, two (2) protoboards, one (1) Arduino Uno, one (1) desktop power supply, and approximately one (1) whole pound of wire, the electrical system will truly shock any reasonable person.

Software

The brain of our project is an Arduino Uno that connects to 5 VL53L0X time-of-flight sensors and 10 servos that turn and bloom the flowers. Check out our more detailed susbsystem page or our GitHub.
