Budget

Throughout the course of our project, we spent less than the allotted $250 budget to purchase the materials we needed to build our archway of flowers. However, we also used items from around the classroom and campus, borrowed items from people, as well as purchased items with our own money for materials that we would keep for our own use. With these items included, the total cost of our entire project experience comes out to $454.

This is efflorescence, a fall 2017 Principles of Engineering project from Olin College of Engineering. The website source code can be found in Louise's GitHub. This is the TXT template from HTML5 UP.
